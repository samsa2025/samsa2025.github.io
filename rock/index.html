Weighted Automata Rock

Problem solving workshop on Series, Automata, Matrices, Symbolic dynamics, and their Applications

Sunday, 27 July, 2025 to Friday, 1 August 2025
European Centre for Geological Education, Chęciny, Poland

Program

The program will consists of informal talks on the details of recent works in weighted automata, and plenty of time to work together on new directions.

The daily schedule and sequencing of talks will be confirmed and agreed with participants on the first day.

Location and getting there:

The workshop takes place in person at European Centre for Geological Education, Chęciny, Poland.

We will arrange a bus to depart from the Faculty of Mathematics, Informatics, and Mechanics, University of Warsaw, at around 4pm on 27/7/25.

The return bus is on Friday afternoon at 13:30.

The travel time is around 2.5 hours

Meals:

All meals will be provided at the centre. If arriving independently on Sunday, please note the dinner will be at 19:00.
